How to use?

  1. Input your username or ID (number in your profile link)
  2. Select your preferred gamemode
  3. Input the pp value of a hypothetical play
  4. If the play is an improved score of a previous play, input its position on your top 100 plays into the 'OVERWRITES' field
  5. Type your API key (get it from here)
  6. Click 'Calculate'
